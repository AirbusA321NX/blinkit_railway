The idea combines AI search, personalization, RAG pipelines, and computer vision to improve user experience and product sales.

Current issue:
Users often don’t know exact product names. They think in needs like:

- “I want something spicy”
- “Need healthy breakfast”
- “Show me what’s in this diet chart”
- “Find this product from photo”

Proposed AI Features:

1. Conversational Smart Search (LLM + RAG Pipeline)
 If a user types “I want spicy”, instead of showing chilli powder first, AI understands intent and retrieves relevant catalog products using a RAG pipeline, then recommends ready-to-eat items such as spicy Maggi, masala noodles, chips, or snacks.

Among young users, many are confused about what to order. Instead of browsing endlessly, they can simply enter intent like:

- “I want something tasty”
- “Need a late night snack”
- “Want quick breakfast”

AI can instantly recommend suitable options.

Future Scope:
By using user age-group, purchase history, and region, recommendations can become hyper-personalized.

Examples:

- User in Bangalore types “I want a tasty snack” → recommends vada, banana chips, local favorites
- User in Punjab types “Need evening snack” → recommends samosa, lassi pairings, namkeen
- Student user types “Cheap filling meal” → budget combos

This can be enhanced further through web search + regional trend signals + local specialties.

2. Personalized Recommendations
 If the user usually buys tea/snacks, recommendations adapt based on purchase history, session behavior, and current intent.

3. Camera / Image Search
 Upload a product photo or empty packet → instantly find the same or similar item for reorder.

4. OCR Smart Cart Builder (Diet Charts / Prescriptions / Medical Terms)
 Upload a doctor prescription, diet chart, handwritten grocery list, or nutrition plan. AI extracts text, understands terms, and recommends relevant products.

Examples:

- “100mg carbohydrates”
- “High fiber diet”
- “Low sodium”
- “Protein supplement”
- oats, almonds, skim milk, banana → cart generated instantly

Business Impact:

- Faster discovery
- Higher conversions
- Better product exposure
- Lower search drop-offs
- Better retention through personalization
- Reduced friction for repeat purchases
